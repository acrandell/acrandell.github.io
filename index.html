About

I'm Anthony, a Game Design and Web Development student at Boise State University, pursuing a degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile (GIMM). My passion lies in creating immersive digital experiences through game design, front-end development, and UX research. With a strong foundation in interactive storytelling, prototyping, and user-centered design, I specialize in building engaging games and web applications that prioritize usability and accessibility. My work often explores player experience, interface design, and the intersection of narrative and mechanics to craft compelling, meaningful interactions. Beyond development, I conduct UX research to improve user engagement and optimize digital experiences, applying usability testing, heuristic evaluation, and player behavior analysis to enhance game and web designs. I'm always looking for opportunities to innovate and collaborate—whether it’s designing games, improving web experiences, or refining UX strategies. Let’s build something great together!
